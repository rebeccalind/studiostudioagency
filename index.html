compact living

Studio Studio specializes in interior design of compact spaces. We offer consultant services for planning and structuring small apartments, just like yours. Our mission is to guide you in how to furnish, style and organize your belongings. In the most efficient way.

We provide a 3D Sketch of the apartment, room or any space you want to optimize. Based on the conditions your home offer and the wishes you have, we adapt our proposition to help create a home that suits you. The furnishment recommendations are carefully selected with your needs and sustainability in mind.

We have made traditional interior designing more seamless, budget friendly and functional by offering a simple and fully customizable alternative that actually works.
